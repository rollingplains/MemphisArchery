May 2–3, 2026

25th Traditional Archery Shoot

Everything visitors need to know for the weekend competition in Memphis, Texas.

Event Highlights

Location: City Park, S 6th Street, Memphis, Texas
Contact: David Tarrant — (806) 204-0184
Saturday: 7:30am–5:00pm
Sunday: 7:30am, cards in by 2:30pm
Courses: Two 30-target courses
Scoring: 12 / 10 / 8 / 5

Traditional Classes

All conventional traditional classes are featured as Course #1.

Primitive Championship

Course #2 includes the Primitive Championship for selfbow with wood arrows.

Open to any archer for $10, including 1 table raffle ticket. MLD Hunt excluded.

Camping & Hookups

Primitive camping is available, with limited camper hookups for water and electric only.

Fees

  • Adults: $25 per class
  • Youth (11–15): $15 per class
  • Cubs and Mini Cubs: Free
  • 3 mulligans included in the fee

Raffle & Extras

  • Tickets available for table raffle items
  • Includes 1 MLD Doe Hunt for 1 hunter
  • Vendors welcome at no charge
